Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Bright Ears

Is this just another YouTube converter?

No! Bright Ears is a professional music discovery tool. We help DJs find the latest releases from their favorite sources and provide one-click search across all major platforms, plus multiple export formats for your workflow.

What makes Bright Ears different?

Unlike simple converters, we focus on latest releases discovery with personalized sources that save across sessions. You get comprehensive CSV exports for track shopping and can filter by timeframe to see only what's new.

Can I add my own YouTube sources?

Yes! Pro users can add unlimited custom YouTube channels and playlists. Your sources are saved permanently, so you can run one-click discoveries whenever you return.

What platforms can I search?

Every track includes one-click search links for Spotify, Tidal, YouTube Music, Beatport, and Traxsource. Plus, export your discoveries as CSV, M3U, or JSON for any other workflow.

How fresh are the "latest releases"?

You control the timeframe! Set anywhere from 1 to 90 days to discover tracks. Most DJs use 7-30 days to catch all new releases without overwhelming their research time.
