Music Discovery Guide

Learn how to find fresh tracks with Bright Ears

Getting Started

Bright Ears helps you discover fresh music from YouTube playlists and export track lists in various formats. Here's how to get the most out of it:

  1. Select your music sources (Pro users)
  2. Choose how far back to search: 1 day, 7 days, or 30 days
  3. Click "Generate Playlist" to start discovering
  4. Export your results in CSV, M3U, or JSON format
  5. Use one-click search links to find tracks on your favorite platform

Export Formats

CSV Format

Perfect for importing into spreadsheets or using for record shopping on Beatport, Juno, etc. Contains artist, title, and source information.

M3U Format

Standard playlist format compatible with most media players. Great for creating local playlists.

JSON Format

Structured data format for developers or advanced users who want to process track data programmatically.
